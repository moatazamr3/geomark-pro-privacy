Privacy Policy

GeoMark Pro by BoraqCom — Effective Date: May 25, 2026

BoraqCom ("we", "us", or "our") built GeoMark Pro as a professional GPS field-data collection application. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and what choices you have.

Summary: GeoMark Pro works entirely offline. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. All data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. GeoMark Pro does not require account creation, login, or registration. The app does not transmit any data to our servers or to third parties.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

GeoMark Pro stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • GPS coordinates — latitude, longitude, altitude, and accuracy readings from your device's GNSS receiver or a connected Bluetooth GPS device.
  • Marked locations — point names, project names, comments, notes, type classifications, and timestamps that you enter.
  • Sketches and photos — drawings and images you attach to marked locations, stored in the app's private storage.
  • Tracks — recorded GPS track logs including position, speed, and time data.
  • App settings — your preferences such as coordinate format, distance units, operator name, and connection settings.

This data is stored in your device's local storage and is never sent to any server unless you explicitly choose to export and share it yourself.

3. Permissions

GeoMark Pro requests the following device permissions:

  • Location (GPS) — required to read your position from the device's built-in GNSS receiver. Used only while the app is in use or during active track recording.
  • Bluetooth — required to connect to external Bluetooth GPS/GNSS receivers for higher-accuracy positioning.
  • Storage / Files — required to export data files (KML, KMZ, GPX, CSV, Excel) and to save/load photos and sketches.
  • Camera (if applicable) — used only when you choose to take a photo for a marked location.
  • Foreground Service / Notifications — used to keep track recording and proximity alerts running when the app is in the background.

All permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality. No data obtained through these permissions is transmitted off-device.

4. Third-Party Services

GeoMark Pro uses OpenStreetMap map tiles for the map display. When you view the map with an active internet connection, your device downloads map tile images from OpenStreetMap tile servers. This is a standard web request that may expose your IP address to the tile server, as with any web-based map. No personal or location data is sent to OpenStreetMap.

The app does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, crash-reporting services, or social-media trackers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties. Your data leaves your device only when you explicitly export it (e.g., saving a KML file, emailing a CSV report, or generating a survey report).

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete individual locations, tracks, or all data at any time from within the app using the "Clear Table" or "Delete" functions. Uninstalling GeoMark Pro removes all app data from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

GeoMark Pro is a professional field-survey tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Security

Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us, the security of your data depends on your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
